# Overlord: Supreme Being - Master Game Design Document

## 1. Executive Summary
**Overlord: Supreme Being** is a hybrid Grand Strategy, Dungeon Simulation, and Action RPG set in the *Overlord* universe. Players assume the role of Ainz Ooal Gown, commanding the Great Tomb of Nazarick to conquer the New World.

## 2. Core Pillars
1.  **Supreme Ruler Fantasy:** Balance overwhelming power with the burden of leadership ("Sasuga Ainz-sama").
2.  **Multilayered Conquest:** Seamlessly switch between strategic world domination, tactical dungeon management, and visceral 3rd-person combat.
3.  **Loyalty & Betrayal:** Manage NPC loyalty through actions, upholding the persona of a genius ruler while secretly fumbling through plans.

## 3. High-Level Game Structure
The game is divided into three distinct layers, each with its own detailed design document.

### [3.1 The Throne Room (Grand Strategy)](mechanics/strategy_layer.md)
*   **Summary:** Turn-based 4X gameplay on a hex map of the New World. Manage diplomacy, espionage, and army movements.
*   **Key Mechanics:** Sasuga Meter, Vassalage vs. Destruction, World Items.

### [3.2 The Tomb (Dungeon Simulation)](mechanics/dungeon_sim.md)
*   **Summary:** Real-time base building and defense of the Great Tomb of Nazarick.
*   **Key Mechanics:** Floor Management, Trap Crafting, resource generation (Scrolls/Potions), defending against Invaders.

### [3.3 The Battlefield (Action RPG)](mechanics/combat_arpg.md)
*   **Summary:** 3rd-person action combat controlling Ainz or Floor Guardians.
*   **Key Mechanics:** Magic System (Tier vs. Super-Tier), "General" Command Interface, Horde vs. Boss combat.
