# Entities: Character Roster & Units

## 1. The Supreme Being: Ainz Ooal Gown
*   **Class:** Overlord (Lvl 100)
*   **Stat Focus:** MP (Infinite-growth potential via items), Magic Attack, Magic Defense.
*   **Special Trait: [The Goal of All Life is Death]**
    *   *Effect:* Once per battle, after a long cooldown, Ainz can ignore all resistances and immunities. Any death spell cast during this window will kill the target instantly, regardless of level.
*   **Skill Tree:** Players can customize Ainz's active spell list (choosing from his 700+ spells) to suit their playstyle (Necromancy, Elemental, Illusion).

## 2. The Floor Guardians (Lvl 100)
Each Guardian has a "Loyalty" stat and a "Combat Specialty."

| Name | Role | Signature Ability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Albedo** | Tank / Commander | **Walls of Jericho:** Unbreakable physical barrier. |
| **Shalltear** | DPS / Healer | **Blood Frenzy:** Increases damage as health drops. |
| **Cocytus** | Melee DPS | **God Slaying Emperor Blade:** Massive linear ice-slash. |
| **Aura** | Ranger / Tamer | **Breath of the Beast:** Buffs all summons in a radius. |
| **Mare** | AOE Mage | **Earthquake:** High-tier AOE stun and damage. |
| **Demiurge** | Support / Summoner | **Command Mantle:** Forces low-level enemies to kneel. |
| **Sebas Tian** | Monk / Assassin | **Internal Strike:** High-crit melee strike that bypasses armor. |

## 3. The Pleiades (Battle Maids)
Mid-tier playable characters (Lvl 50-60). Used for smaller-scale "Ops" missions.
*   **Yuri Alpha (Leader):** Striker / Monk.
*   **Lupusregina Beta:** Battle Cleric / Pyromancer.
*   **Narberal Gamma:** Battle Mage (Chain Lightning specialist).
*   **CZ2128 Delta:** Gunner (Long-range physical).
*   **Solution Epsilon:** Assassin (Slime-based crowd control).
*   **Entoma Vasilissa Zeta:** Entomancer (Summons insects, debuffer).

## 4. Nazarick Summoned Units (The Army)
These are the units players command in the Strategy and ARPG layers.

*   **Low Tier (Lvl 1-30):**
    *   *Skeletons/Zombies:* Free to spawn from corpses. Weak fodder.
    *   *Elder Liches:* Basic magic support.
*   **Mid Tier (Lvl 35-60):**
    *   **Death Knights:** The backbone of the army. High HP, "Shield of the Dead" (can't be one-shot), and "Create Lesser Undead" (kills create zombies).
    *   **Soul Eaters:** Fast cavalry that heals by "eating" the morale of nearby enemies.
*   **High Tier (Lvl 80+):**
    *   **Hanzo (Shadow Demon):** Perfect for espionage and assassinations.
    *   **Eternal Deaths:** Elite undead mages.
    *   **Primal Elementals:** Massive elemental summons for sieges.

## 5. New World "Hero" Entities (The Enemy)
*   **Gazef Stronoff:** High physical defense, martial arts user.
*   **Brain Unglaus:** Precision striker.
*   **Fluder Paradyne:** Tier 6 magic user (can be recruited/vassalized).
*   **The Black Scripture:** The Theocracy's elite. Level 60-80, equipped with YGGDRASIL artifacts.
*   **The Platinum Dragon Lord (True Dragon):** The ultimate boss. Uses Wild Magic and soul-based attacks.
